Frequently Asked Questions (Q&A)

Does Super-C offer service and maintenance?

Yes, we have a state-of-the-art service facility dedicated to supercars. Our technicians are factory-certified to handle your vehicle with the utmost care.

What is the process for international import/export?

We provide a full white-glove service for sourcing and importing CBU (Completely Built Up) units globally, handling all logistics, documentation, and taxes to your door.

Can I trade-in my current vehicle?

Absolutely. We accept trade-ins of other supercars or luxury vehicles. Our team will conduct a thorough inspection and provide a competitive, market-based offer for your unit.
